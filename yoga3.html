Here is Yoga Position #3

Cobra Pose

Step #1: Lying flat on your stomach, place your hands on the side and ensure that your toes touch each other.

Step #2: Then, move your hands to the front and place your palms on the floor and place your body's weight on your palms. Raise your head.

Step #3: Arch your neck backward to copy the cobra with the raised hood. Make sure your shoulder blades are firm, and your shoulders are away from your ears.

Step #4: Press your hips, thighs, and feet to the floor.

Step #5: Hold the stance for about 15 to 30 seconds while breathing normally. Feel your stomach pressed against the floor.

Release the pose by slowly bring your hands back to the sides. Rest your head on the ground by bringing your forehead touching the floor. Place your hands under your head. Then, slowly rest your head on one side and breathe.
